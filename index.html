Unit 1: Creating Pages with HTML

  1. What do you think are the most important vocabulary terms (or key concepts) in this unit? What do you think that they mean?
    • I think that the important vocabulary terms would be head, tags, body, html.
  2. What do you think are the most important skills that you learned in this unit of study?
    • The most important skills would be to remember what style you're going to use for the website.
  3. What do you think are the most important skills that you learned in this unit of study?
    • In ways of getting better were to use the right codes for the web page.
  4. In what ways do you think you need to improve?
    • In ways of improving the work are to use the codes properly for its use.
  5. What problems did you encounter while you were working on this piece? How did you solve them?
    • Problems that I've encountered for this work were what kinds of codes to be put in. I was able to fix the problem of asking help.
  6. What is satisfying me for this project is that there are codes that are usesd to create a website not just words being typed.
  7. If I were to be teacher and review over this product, there will be mistakes that will be shown, but there's enough time to fix the mistake for the project to be completed.
